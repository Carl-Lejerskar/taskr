taskr - A command line to-do list.
----------------------------------

MVP:

1. taskr push "Go for a run"
   taskr push "Do 5 LC Easy's"
   taskr push "Write a short story"

2. taskr list
   
   1. Go for a run
   2. Do 5 LC Easy's
   3. Write a short story

3. taskr finished 1

4. taskr list

   1. Do 5 LC Easy's 
   2. Write a short story

5. taskr progress

   1 completed

6. taskr remove 1

6. taskr add-daily-task "Exercise."

   12:01 AM on any day following the above command:
   
   taskr list

   1. Exercise

7. tasker list-daily-tasks

   1. Exercise
